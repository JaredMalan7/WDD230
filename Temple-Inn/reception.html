Reception Venue

Our venue is available for receptions, ring and civil wedding ceremonies. However, civil weddings can only be scheduled if the couple has a live sealing session scheduled with the temple, and must provide proof of such along with a marriage license. Civil weddings can be hosted no earlier than 24 hours before the temple ceremony. It is advised that civil weddings maybe scheduled just before the temple sealing, but in the same day.

Catering

Temple Inn & Suites has a large variety of options for catering to meet all needs. All menu options and prices can be found on the catering div of the site. Making use of our catering services is not required in order to schedule our venue. However, different packages and prices based on number of guests are available.
